Understand the meaning of Bike Insurance for Third Parties!

Our third-party bike insurance offers economic security for third-party losses incurred as a result of accidents involving the insured two-wheeler. These losses include third-party injuries and property damage. Additionally, if a third party is killed in an accident caused by the licensed two-wheeler, it provides you with legal or financial protection.

All two-wheeler owners are required by the Motor Vehicles Act of 1988 to maintain third-party bike insurance.
